Privacy Policy for Meditation Timer App Wear OS

This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled in the Meditation Timer App Wear OS application, developed by MarCas Code.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at: marcascode@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

Meditation Timer App Wear OS does not require user registration and does not directly collect personally identifiable information.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect limited data:

a) Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to understand how the app is used.

  • Device information
  • App interactions (e.g., session duration, button taps)
  • General location (country-level, not precise)

This data is anonymized and used only to improve the app experience.

b) In-App Purchases

The app uses Google Play Billing to process purchases.

We do not store or have access to your payment details. All transactions are handled securely by Google.

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand feature usage
  • Fix bugs and crashes

3. Data Retention

Meditation Timer App Wear OS does not store personal data on its own servers.

Analytics data is collected and processed by Firebase, a service provided by Google. This analytics data is retained for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted by Google.

We do not control the exact retention period beyond the configuration provided by Firebase.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data.

Data may be processed by trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (analytics)
  • Google Play (payments)

5. Children’s Privacy

Meditation Timer App Wear OS is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Security

We rely on trusted platforms such as Google Play Services and Firebase to ensure data is handled securely.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.

8. Consent

By using Meditation Timer App Wear OS, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

9. Data Deletion

Meditation Timer App Wear OS does not store personal data directly on its own servers.

If you wish to stop further data collection, you can uninstall the app at any time.

For analytics data collected through Firebase, you can manage or delete your data through your Google account privacy settings: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy

You may also reset your device’s advertising ID to limit data collection.

If you have any specific data-related requests, you can contact us at: marcascode@gmail.com

We will make reasonable efforts to assist you.
